Factors Affecting Coral Presence in the Arabian Sea

Introduction

About Corals

  • Corals are the foundation of our oceans food chain.
  • Healthy coral reefs contribute to fishing and tourism, providing millions of jobs and contributing to economies all over the world.
  • Scientists develop important drugs from coral reef organisms as treatments for cancer and viruses.
  • Corals are threatened by pollution and climate change.

My website will analyse the factors that can affect the distribution of corals in sea. Factors that determine the presence or absence of corals are controlled by human activity. Our human activity causes pollution levels to increase and climate change to occur quicker.

Seeing as corals are present and thriving in the Arabian Sea, the factors of the environment can be copied and possibly implemented into other seas. This may be done by reducing pollution and slowing down the rate of climate change. Hopefully, my website will assist in indicating what humanity can do to save coral reefs ecosystem. Together, maybe we can prevent the death of coral reefs in the future.
